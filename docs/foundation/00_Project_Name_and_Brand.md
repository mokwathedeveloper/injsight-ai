## Why This Name Works

InjSight AI is short, unique, and easy to remember. The name combines **Injective** with **insight**, which clearly communicates the purpose of the project: helping users understand Injective wallet and portfolio data through AI-powered analysis.

It also sounds like a real product rather than just a basic hackathon submission, making it more polished and memorable for judges, users, and future contributors.
